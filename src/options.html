Cineville Ratings

Letterboxd ratings work out of the box. IMDb ratings are optional and need a free OMDb API key.

Get a free key at omdbapi.com/apikey.aspx (1,000 requests/day). Stored only in your browser, never committed to the repo.

Ratings are cached for speed. Clear the cache to force a fresh lookup (e.g. after adding your OMDb key or upgrading the extension).
